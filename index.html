The greatest rapper of all time

Here's a time line of 2pac shakur life:

  • 1971 -Born in manhattan New York
  • 1984 -Relocated to Maryland
  • 1988 -Relocated to san francisco
  • 1993 - He moved to Los Angeles to further pursue his music career
  • 1993 - He starred alongside Janet Jackson in a movie called lucky in poetic justice,
  • 1995 - He served 8 months in prison for sexual assault charges
  • 1996 -His double-disc album All Eyez on Me was certified Diamond by the RIAA
  • 1996 -He died at the age of 26. he was shot three times and died six day later and the gunmen were never capture
  • 2017 -He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in his first year of eligibility.
Shakur is one of the best-selling music artists of all time, having sold over 75 million records worldwide. In 2002, he was inducted into the Hip-Hop Hall of Fame. In 2017, he was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in his first year of eligibility. Rolling Stone named Shakur in its list of the 100 Greatest Artists of All Time.
